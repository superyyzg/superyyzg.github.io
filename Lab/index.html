Statistical Deep Learning (SDL) Lab

General Goal

In SDL, we focus on both theoretical foundation of machine learning/deep learning and theory-inspired applications to broad aspects of deep learning, including transformers, graph learning, neural architecture search, model compression and edge computing, etc.
